Lasagna Recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but he results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below

Ingredients

  • Meat: Typically we use sweet italian sausage and lean groud beef.
  • Onion and garlic: one onion and two cloves of garlic add tons fo flavor to the dish.
  • Tomato products: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste is all we need.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper makes the lasagna recipe flavored.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-brought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta cheese make the lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
